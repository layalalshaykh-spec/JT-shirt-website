## "The cost you don't see" (4 hidden cost categories)
1. Frequent replacements (low-grade fabric fails in months)
2. Quality inconsistency (off-shade fabric, wrong fit, missing logo)
3. Administrative overhead (HR/Ops tracking sizes by spreadsheet)
4. Brand dilution (faded logos, mismatched colors across sites)

## Configurator feature
Live uniform builder: garment (T-Shirt / Polo / Work Shirt / Jacket / Cap) →
color (hex swatch) → logo upload (PNG/JPG/SVG, max 5MB; left chest / right chest /
center) → quantity (min 25) → live unit price + estimated total. T-shirt renders in
real 3D (drag to rotate, scroll to zoom); other garments as editorial mockups.
